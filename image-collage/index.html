How to Add Headers and Footers to a PDF

Adding a header or footer lets you stamp crucial information like company names, copyright notices, dates, or page numbers directly onto the margins of your PDF. This tool runs 100% in your browser. Select a file, customize the text for the header (top margin) or footer (bottom margin), and hit Add. Placeholders like {n} and {total} are dynamically calculated for each page.

Header & Footer — Frequently Asked Questions

Can I add both header and footer at the same time?

Yes. You can enter text in both the Header and Footer input fields, and the tool will stamp both simultaneously.

How do I add page numbers?

You can use the placeholder {n} to display the page number, or Page {n} of {total} to show the page number along with the total count (e.g. Page 1 of 5).

Is my document uploaded to a server?

No. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device.
