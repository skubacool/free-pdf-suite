How to Compare PDF Text

Comparing two documents line-by-line is essential in legal, academic, and business settings. The PDF Text Diff tool extracts all textual content from two PDF documents (Original and Modified) and runs a programmatic comparison. Added text is highlighted in bold green, and deleted text is highlighted in strike-through red, making changes instantly visible.

PDF Text Diff — Frequently Asked Questions

Does this compare images or layout changes?

No. This is a text-based diff tool. For comparing visual differences or layout offsets, use the main Compare PDFs tool.

What types of PDFs are supported?

It supports any PDF containing selectable text. Scanned PDFs without OCR text will not have extractable text for comparison.

Is my document uploaded to a server?

No. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device.
