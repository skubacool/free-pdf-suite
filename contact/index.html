A quick note on your files

Because every tool runs entirely in your browser, we never receive, see, or store the documents you process — so we can't look up "your file" or recover one for you. If you're reporting a bug with a specific document, please don't email us the sensitive file itself; a description of the problem (and, if possible, a non-sensitive sample that reproduces it) is all we need.

Who you're reaching

Free PDF Suite is an independent project. Messages go directly to the founder and maintainer — there's no ticket queue or outsourced call centre between you and the person who builds the tools. Learn more on our About page.
