Background gone, privacy intact

Removing a background usually means uploading your photo to someone else's server. This tool runs a background-removal AI model directly in your browser, so your image - a product shot, a portrait, an ID photo - never leaves your device.

The result is a PNG with a transparent background, ready to drop onto any colour, a new scene, or a white card for a passport photo.

How to use it

  1. Drop a photo with a clear subject.
  2. Wait while the model loads (first time only) and processes the image.
  3. Download the transparent PNG, or pass it to the Passport Photo tool to add a white background.
🔒 100%% private. Your images are processed entirely in your browser - they are never uploaded to any server.

Frequently asked questions

Does it really work offline / privately?

The model and your photo are processed in your browser. After the one-time model download, no image data is sent anywhere.

Why is the first run slow?

It downloads the AI model the first time (tens of MB). Subsequent images are much faster as the model is cached.

What works best?

Photos with a clear subject and reasonable contrast against the background give the cleanest cut-outs.
