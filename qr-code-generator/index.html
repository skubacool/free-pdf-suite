How to Generate and Stamp QR Codes on a PDF

A QR code allows readers to scan a paper or digital document with their phone and quickly access an external URL, digital form, or payment link. This tool lets you type a URL or plain text to instantly generate a QR code. You can then download the QR image directly, or upload a PDF to automatically overlay the QR code onto the top-right corner of the first page.

QR Code Generator — Frequently Asked Questions

Can I download the QR code without a PDF?

Yes. Just enter your text or link, see the live preview, and click Generate. You can then download the standalone QR code image as a PNG file.

Where on the PDF is the QR code placed?

If you upload a PDF, the QR code is automatically placed in the top-right corner of the first page, sized cleanly at 100x100 pixels so it doesn't block main content.

Is my document uploaded to a server?

No. Everything runs locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device.
