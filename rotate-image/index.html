Straighten or mirror in one click

Phones and cameras sometimes save photos with the wrong orientation, or you simply need a mirrored version. This tool rotates an image in 90-degree steps or flips it along either axis, keeping full quality.

The output keeps your original format (JPG, PNG or WebP), and the whole operation happens on your device.

How to use it

  1. Drop your image.
  2. Choose rotate (90/180/270) or flip (horizontal/vertical).
  3. Click Apply and download.
🔒 100%% private. Your images are processed entirely in your browser - they are never uploaded to any server.

Frequently asked questions

Does rotating lose quality?

No meaningful loss - the pixels are redrawn at full resolution. JPG output is re-saved at high quality; PNG and WebP stay lossless.

What's the difference between flip and rotate?

Rotate turns the image around its centre; flip mirrors it, like a reflection. Use flip horizontal to mirror left-right.

Are my images uploaded?

No. Everything runs in your browser, so your images stay on your device.
